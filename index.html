About Us

Geocelestial Ltd is a registered company in Kenya offering professional services in land surveying, Geospatial Information Systems, Remote Sensing, Environmental Impact Assessment, Environmental Audit, and Information Technology. We meet this broad spectrum of surveying and engineering needs by offering high-quality work within reliable turnaround timelines, and in unparalleled professionalism.

Our Services

Land Surveying

Our land surveyors are registered with the Institution of Surveyors of Kenya (ISK) and the Land Surveyors Board (LSB) to provide Cadastral/Real Property, Topographical, Engineering, Mining, and Hydrographic Surveying services to clients countrywide.

GIS

Our experienced personnel in the GIS department conduct Spatial Analysis and Visualization of various datasets, predicting the outcomes of different actions to help clients make informed decisions.

Remote Sensing

Our partnerships with global data collectors like NASA, USGS, Kenya Space Agency, among others; gives us access to a wide range of remotely acquired satellite data. Moreover, our team in partnership with KCAA has been licensed to operate Unmanned Aerial Vehicles/ Drones; facilitating fast massive data collection as well as in inaccessible areas.

Environmental Impact Assessment

All development projects require EIA/EA. Our experts, licensed by NEMA, conduct audits and generate comprehensive EIA reports for various projects.

Information Technology (IT)

Our tech enthusiasts through machine learning, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and Programming languages have the ability to develop Land Information Systems, Web Maps, GIS Systems, for organizations.

GIS Training

We offer comprehensive GIS training programs designed to equip individuals with the skills needed to excel in spatial data management, mapping, and analysis. Our training courses cover GIS software, remote sensing, and spatial data analysis techniques.
